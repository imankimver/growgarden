Zen Event

The Zen Event is part of Update 1.15.0 and is similar to the Bizzy Bee Event and the Lunar Glow Event.

← Back to Blog

During the Zen Aura Weather in Grow a Garden, players have the unique opportunity to trade their Tranquil crops for Chi by giving them to either the Zen Channeller or the Tanuki NPC. This makes collecting Tranquil crops highly valuable for progressing in the Zen Event. The Tranquil mutation itself occurs randomly, transforming a standard crop into a Tranquil crop every 30 seconds while Zen Aura Weather is active in your garden. To maximize Chi earnings, it’s important to maintain a steady supply of crops ready for mutation during this event. This mechanic encourages players to actively manage their garden and optimize their crop production cycles.

Zen Channeller

The Zen Channeller converts Tranquil crops into Chi, which is instantly used to gradually grow the Zen Tree through seven stages. Each stage unlocks its corresponding item in the Tranquil Treasures Shop and grants various rewards. These rewards can include:

Rewards
x1-5 Zen Egg
x1-3 Zen Seed Pack
1x Zen Crate
1x Zen Gnome Crate
20 Chi

Each stage of the Zen Tree requires a specific amount of Chi to level up. After unlocking all shop items at the final 7th stage, players can still continue leveling up the tree to earn extra rewards. Although the Zen Tree does not grow beyond stage 7, ongoing progress will still grant valuable rewards.

Tanuki and Tranquil Treasures Shop

Tanuki is the merchant responsible for converting Tranquil crops into Chi, which serves as the currency for the Tranquil Treasures Shop, also operated by Tanuki. Players can exchange Tranquil crops with Tanuki in a similar way as they do with the Zen Channeller, but instead of contributing to tree growth, the Chi earned is saved for use in the shop.

The Tranquil Treasures Shop refreshes its stock every 60 minutes automatically. Players also have the option to manually restock the shop by paying 500,000 Sheckles, with each subsequent manual restock doubling in cost using the formula: 500,000 × 2^(Manual Restocks)

Weather

The Zen Aura weather closely resembles the weather effects seen in the Bee Event and Night Event. It appears every 60 minutes and lasts for 10 minutes, changing the sky’s appearance and softening the ambient lighting to create a calm atmosphere.

During Zen Aura, crops have a 3.5% chance every second to develop the Tranquil mutation, resulting in an average of 10 Tranquil-mutated crops per Zen Aura cycle. These special crops can then be collected and exchanged with either the Zen Channeller or Tanuki to serve their respective purposes in the event.
